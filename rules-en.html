House rules

Home respect

Our flat does not charge additional cleaning fees.

We expect our guests to respect the furnishings and linen.

Avoid leaving food leftovers in rooms, especially in summer

When checking out, do not leave any rubbish strewn about.

Report promptly if there are faults or defects in furniture and/or electrical appliances.

Standards of responsible behaviour

To avoid food waste, eat the food you cook

The well-insulated house heats and cools quickly and maintains the desired temperature for a long time.

Switch off heating systems when you are not at home.

Do not use air conditioners and/or heating with open windows.

Close the windows when going out.

Temperature in environments

Winter

MAX 23°C, ideal 19-21°C

Summer

MIN 21*C, ideal 22-25°C

Separate collection and waste management

Comply with the instructions for separate collection.

https://palgu.github.io/rubbish/

To avoid unpleasant odours and unwelcome insects (ants, flies) move the waste to the outdoor storage area daily by closing the containers.

When checking out, move the rubbish to the outside storage area.
